## Key Findings

### 1. High Editing Accuracy
- The model successfully learned **83.4%** of the requested edits on average
- Rephrase accuracy of **90.6%** shows strong generalization to paraphrased prompts

### 2. Stable Performance
- Accuracy remained relatively stable throughout all 986 edits
- No catastrophic forgetting observed

### 3. Efficient Convergence
- Loss decreased by 90.5% from first to last edit
- Median loss of 0.0046 indicates consistent convergence

### 4. Good Locality Preservation
- **70.7%** locality means the model retained most of its original knowledge
- Unrelated facts were not significantly affected by edits
